What it does ?

WallPbot is a major technical advancement in automated wall painting that reduces human casualties and chemical hazards caused by exposure to paint. Professional painters are most at risk as they have a 20% increased risk of lung cancer, according to the WHO.

Responsive Design

WallPbot makes use of a Rack and Pinion gear system coupled to stepper motors for high precision vertical movement.Horizontal movement using sensor aligned top and bottom modules.

How is it different

Most of the implemented systems are wheeled robots with robotic hands or otherwise a frame suspended robot using spray painters but the efficiency of the robot is very less even when compared to a human counterpart.

Powerful.

Wallpbot uses a stepper motor drive with 4 dedicated motors coupled with rack and pinion gears to give the robot a smooth and precise motion.

Autonomous

The robot can work on a fully autonomous mode wherein it will decide the area that needs to be painted and plan its path accordings and paints the surface with no human interaction.

Our Journey

Our Humble Beginnings

On a bus ride back home I saw a man hanging from a 10 story building holding a roller painting the walls in the hot sun. I made up a team and did some research on the topic and found out that there were no devices existing that solves this.

Design process & prototyping

In one month time we went from design to prototype wherein we simultaneously modeled it in Sketch up and made the prototype from scratch gathering all the components like motors, rollers, support boards and controllers. We had our successful testing done on our system wherein it climbed a rope and the rollers painted the wall with ease. The prototype at first was only a rope climbing mechanism we perfected it very well then we moved on to creating the roller dispenser module ,most of the parts were gathered from scratch.

Future Plans

Using Machine Learning and path planning algorithms to map the entire area of the building and work the robot in a fully autonomous mode. In a simple way press "START" and relax, while it automatically figures out the best coat and thickness of paint and plans its path over the area that needs to be covered by paint and finishes the job quick.

Awards

After a lot of hardwork we were able to succesfully apply for an Indian Patent .Confidential Indian Patent Filed 201841026423.Participated in James Dyson Award International Design competition.Finalist Accenture innovation challenge.

WallPbot is programmed intelligently to ensure there is minimal human interaction.

To eradicate adverse medical conditions and hazards caused by inhalation of harmful paint fumes at the cost of electricity.As a bot that can potentially replace humans who, out of their bonds to profession, are required to daringly put their lives at the edge in an attempt to complete the task of just applying a coat of paint on tall walls.

About Us

A group of four B.Tech students of Electronics and Communication Department Amritapuri Campus has joined hands to come up with an intelligent wall climbing painter robot which has already begun its journey to revolt the painting industry. Harikrishnan J, Aravind Sadashiv, Akhil Suryakiran and Arya Sudarsan of Amrita School of Engineering Kollam has brought to light a robot which ascends tall walls upon which it gets mounted, independently, and simultaneously paints the surface without compromising the finish. As per Aravind, WallPbot is an algorithmically programmed and specially designed instrumental piece of advanced technology to conquer heights that are humanely difficult to pull-off and paint, thereby, significantly reduces human casualties and labour. The Phase 1 prototype was successfully built and tested under the mentorship of Dr. Purushothaman A at the Technology Business Incubator (TBI) of Amritapuri Campus. Currently, they have a confidential Indian patent pending.
